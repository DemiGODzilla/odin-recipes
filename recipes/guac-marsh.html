Guacamole Marshmellow

Description

¡Olé! This delicious pizza combines the Italian pie with the famed Mexican dip, and then adds a touch of sweetness with the marshmellows. ¡Perfecto!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • guacamole
  • marshmellow
  • 8oz mozzarella
  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees
  2. Stretch pizza dough to 12 inch circle.
  3. Add a nice heaping of our homemade sauce. Spread evenly across the dough
  4. Cover the pizza with mozzarella.
  5. Add spoonfuls of guacamole
  6. Bake for 10 minutes
  7. Add marshmellows to pizza and bake for another 5-10 minutes (until the crust is golden brown)
