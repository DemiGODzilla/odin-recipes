Anchovy and Banana Pizza

Description

Sweet, salty, fishy. These words describe the perfect flavor combination for any pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • 1 can anchovies
  • 1 banana
  • 8oz mozzarella
  1. preheat oven to 475 degrees
  2. Stretch pizza dough to 12 inch circle.
  3. Add a nice heaping of our homemade sauce. Spread evenly across the dough
  4. Generously add anchovies and sliced banana
  5. Cover pizza with mozzarella
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
